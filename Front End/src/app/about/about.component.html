There are more than 44,000 accredited programs available in the USA for prospective undergraduate students to choose from. Whose, program contents are often intricate and difficult for high school students to comprehend. Nearly 15.9 million undergraduate students enroll yearly at universities, out of which it is observed that 30 percent of the students switch their major within the first three years of enrollment. Due to the lack of cohesive tools to serve as their guides, students make ill-informed decisions that leads to these miscalculations. We would like to improve this situation by our machine-learning tool - PROGRAM DECISION SUPPORT TOOL which will support prospective students in selecting the right program through a data-driven approach.
